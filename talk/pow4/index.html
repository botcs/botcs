"What I cannot create, I do not understand"

Abstract

Brain experiments are pretty expensive, or at least unethical but for example we can train a classifier to recognise objects of any arbitrary classes and peek inside the resulting visual cortex without damaging them. The question is: do these neural networks learn the same representations as humans? Definitely not!

What about training them to use the same encoding as the human visual, so we don’t have to crack our skulls to know what is going on inside. Generative methods could help us deciphering our brain’s language, so we can actually see through someone else’s eye…

Are we actually living the golden age of AI breakthroughs?
